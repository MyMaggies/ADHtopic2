This interactive tool will help you analyze the social and historical context of Henrik Ibsen's famous play, A Doll's House.

The Essay Prompt

  • Henrik Ibsen's "A Doll's House" was written in the 19th century.
  • This was a time when women were expected to conform to strict gender roles dictated by society.
  • Your goal: Explore how the play reflects the social and historical context of this era.
  • Discuss the societal norms and expectations placed on women.
  • Consider how Nora's character embodies these societal constraints and how her actions in the play challenge and subvert these norms.
  • Support your analysis with textual evidence.

Proceed to the Quiz tab to test your knowledge before building your essay!
